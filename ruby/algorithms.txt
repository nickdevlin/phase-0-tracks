Elevator robot:

- Wait until elevator door opens.
- Enter elevator.
- Scan for button panel to left or right of door.
- Orient body toward button panel.
- Scan buttons.
	- If button does not equal 16, continue scanning.
	- If button equals 16, push.
- When elevator arrives at 16, exit elevator.

Taking out trash:

- Open trash can
- If height of trash is further than three inches from top rim of trash can, close trash can.
- Otherwise:
	- Remove trash bag from trash can.
	- Tie bag shut and set aside.
	- Retrieve empty bag from beneath sink.
	- Place empty bag in trash can.
	- Close trash can.
	- Take full trash bag to garbage can outdoors.
